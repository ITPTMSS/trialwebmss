About Catalyst Handling

At MSS Catalyst, catalyst handling is a core service that underscores our commitment to safety, quality, and precision. Our team is highly skilled in managing the complex and demanding process of catalyst change-out, which is critical in various industries such as petrochemicals, refineries, and other process plants.

Services Offered

  • Execution planning
  • Blind to blind catalyst change-out services
  • Catalyst loading and unloading under inert or normal conditions
  • Wet or dry catalyst removal options
  • Dense Loading and Tube Loading Systems
  • Services in hazardous, toxic and inert environments
  • Vessel internal modifications and revamps
  • Vacuum unloading with closed loop nitrogen re-circulation
  • Catalyst screening and catalyst oxidisation
  • Pre-commissioning of reactors and vessels and pre-sulphiding
  • Mercury detection, handling and sampling
  • Spent adsorbent handling and waste processing
  • Transportation, storage and containment (UN-approved containers)
